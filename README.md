**Ever wondered how much durability your enemy's armor or weapons have? If so, GearTags got you Covered!**

![Example Usage](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/0bb66426dd726ead5beadfa32739c5157d1ffdbd.png)

**GearTags** is a lightweight, **client-sided** fabric mod that displays other players **Armor** and **Hand Items** durability / count directly above them just like nametags **giving you the edge in battles**.

---

### ‚ú® Features

- **In-world gear overlays**  
  - Shows what armor and weapons other players have equipped.  
  - Uses **Color-Coded durability text** so you can instantly distinguish gear durability.  
  - Works seamlessly on **almost all servers** (vanilla or modded) since it‚Äôs fully client-side.

- **Configurable display**  
  - Easily tweak **Overlay, Item Icon & Text** visuals. 

- **Simple commands & menus**  
  - `/geartags` ‚Äî Open the GearTags config screen directly in-game.  
  - **Mod Menu integration** ‚Äî Configure settings through Mod Menu if installed.

- **Lightweight & flexible**  
  - Renders efficiently without lag.  
  - Perfect for PvP & Co-Operative Play.
- **Toggle Keybind**
  - Supports a keybind to toggle the Overlay.
  - Configurable under vanilla controls settings in **miscellaneous** category.
  - **Default Keybind:** `none`
